AEROCORE

PROJECT INFO

The brief for this project was to design assets and market the release of a new graphics card for an online company. I used adobe photoshop and illustrator to design Logo's, Typeface, brochure wallpapers and inital characters, Then I used blender to bring my characters to the 3d space and improve them.
The graphics card was 3d modelled from scratch and then overlayed with texturing done in photoshop, Lastly I created an animation showcasing the card with cinematic lighting, this was the release trailer.




Programs Used:
Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe After Effects, Davinci Resolve, Blender.
