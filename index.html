About me

Hey Im Eben, A designer and visual creative with a focus on graphic design, 3D, film, and photography. Back in high school, I was awarded a graphic design scholarship for a marketing project I built from scratch, from character design and assets creation to a 3D animated ad. Since then, I’ve been sharpening my skills across Photoshop, Blender, and the front-end web. Outside of design, I run CineMagnet, where I shoot cinematic car videos and clean automotive photography, bringing that same attention to detail to color, motion, and composition.
